7 Steps Every Patient Should Take
  1. Understand your diagnosis.
  2. Keep a running list of questions and concerns.
  3. Ask a family member to accompany you to your appointments.
  4. Learn about all your treatment options.
  5. Get a second opinion.
  6. Review your insurance coverage.
  7. Get support
Key Terminology
Benign
Any tumor that’s not cancerous.
Biopsy
Removal of a small portion of tissue to determine if it’s cancerous.
Carcinoma
Cancer disease that begins in the skin or organ tissues. Learn more about skin cancer.
Cells
Your body’s smallest functional unit. The human body is made of trillions of cells.
